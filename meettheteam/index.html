Meet the Team

JavaJokers is one of the 2024-2025 First Tech Challenge (FTC) team for Golda Meir School, located in Milwaukee, WI.

We are a team of 4 people, and our goal is to expand our and others current knowledge of robotics, marketing and outreach, coding and app development, and much more.
