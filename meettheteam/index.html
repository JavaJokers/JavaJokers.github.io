Meet the Team

JavaJokers is the 2021-2022 First Tech Challenge (FTC) team for the Golda Meir School, located in Milwaukee, WI.

We are a team of 15 people, and our goal is to expand our current knowledge of robotics, marketing and outreach, coding and app development, and much more.
Our team number is 13828.
